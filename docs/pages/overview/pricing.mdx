## Pricing

Tenderly offers a pricing plan tailored for every stage of your product. Its plans cover **Free, Gateway, Dev, Pro, and Pro+**.  
The free plan comes with Forks, Visual Debugger, 
Transaction Overview (State Changes, Decoded Events, Tokens Transferred), 
Advanced Trace Search, and Tenderly Web3 Gateway Free Tier. etc. 
Learn more about [Tenderly Pricing](https://tenderly.co/pricing).
